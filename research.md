# Research and Projects

### Methods for applying 2D architectural styles to 3D architectural models
**UCL, The Bartlett School of Architecture - MEng Dissertation (2023)**
The project explores the feasability of applying 2D architectural features to 3D models using Neural Style Transfer (NST) and Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs).

**Highlights:**
- Implemented 2D-to-3D and multi-view style transfer using Tensorflow and VGG-19.
- Integrated MiDaS depth estimation to improve texture mapping.
- Tested GAN-based and hybrid models for architectural visualisation and heritage conservation.
- Tools used: TensorFlow, Mayavi, Agisoft Metashape, Adobe Substance 3D, Python.
